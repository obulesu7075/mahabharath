Karna

Weapon

Skill

Karna was an unmatched archer, warrior, and strategist. Trained by the great sage Parashurama, he mastered all forms of combat, especially archery. He possessed immense strength, courage, and resilience. Despite facing humiliation for his low birth, he never gave up his pursuit of excellence. Karna’s loyalty, charity, and unwavering spirit earned him the title of *Danveer* — the greatest giver.

War

In the Kurukshetra war, Karna fought on the side of the Kauravas out of loyalty to Duryodhana, who accepted him when the world rejected him. He served as the commander of the Kaurava army after Dronacharya’s fall. On the 17th day of the war, he faced his greatest rival — Arjuna. Though destiny and fate were against him, Karna fought valiantly until the very end. He was killed when his chariot wheel got stuck in the mud, making him vulnerable to Arjuna’s arrow guided by Lord Krishna.

Character

Karna’s life was marked by honor, generosity, and tragedy. He was born to Kunti and the Sun God (Surya) but was abandoned at birth and raised by a humble charioteer family. Despite being denied respect for his true worth, Karna upheld his dignity and principles. His unwavering friendship with Duryodhana, his unmatched charity, and his adherence to his word even in death make him one of the noblest figures in the epic.
